Welcome to TriQuoWik

This is a Game that runs like Trivia. Selecting your field of interests first and random questions will pop up. You can answer them like you are acing them. Sometimes there will be open/ambiguous question such as "What is life?". In this case, you will need to write paragraph about what you know. For question like these, we will not judge your answer like a math problem. Your answer will be collected first and then suggested answers will be provided. These answers are mainly from Quora, Wikipedia or sometimes papers from professor in well-known field. You answer will be reviewed manually by the developer and they will perfect the answer to the specific question if your answer is awesome. All answers are only for reference.
